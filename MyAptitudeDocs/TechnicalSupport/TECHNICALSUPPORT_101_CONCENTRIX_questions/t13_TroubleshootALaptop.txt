How to trouble shoot when a laptop is overcharged and is not turning on?
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Disconnect from Power Source:
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Unplug the laptop from the power adapter and remove the battery if possible. This ensures no power is being supplied to the laptop.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Wait and Cool Down:
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Allow the laptop to cool down for some time. Overheating can cause temporary malfunctions, and letting it cool may resolve the issue.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Inspect for Physical Damage:
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Check the power adapter, charging port, and battery for any signs of physical damage, such as frayed cables, bent pins, or bulging battery.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Reset Power Management:
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Press and hold the power button for 15-20 seconds to drain any residual power and reset the power management system.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Test with Another Power Adapter:
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
If available, try using another compatible power adapter to rule out any issues with the original adapter.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Test with Another Battery (if possible):
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
If the laptop has a removable battery, try using another compatible battery to see if the issue is with the battery itself.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Check Indicator Lights:
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Look for any indicator lights on the laptop that may provide clues about its status or any potential errors.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Perform a Hard Reset:
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Remove the battery and power adapter.
Press and hold the power button for 30 seconds.
Reconnect the power adapter (without the battery) and try turning on the laptop.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Inspect Internal Components:
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
If comfortable and knowledgeable about laptop internals, consider opening the laptop to inspect internal components such as the motherboard, 
RAM, and power connectors for any signs of damage or loose connections.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Seek Professional Assistance:
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
If none of the above steps resolves the issue, it may indicate a more serious hardware problem. In such cases, it's best to seek assistance from a professional technician or contact the laptop manufacturer's support for further diagnosis and repair.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Caution:
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Be cautious when working with electronic devices, especially when opening them up or dealing with electrical components.
If unsure about performing any steps, it's advisable to seek assistance from a professional technician to avoid causing further 
damage to the laptop.
